Yield: 8x8 Tray

Ingredients

1 cup (226 g) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups (400 g) granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup (75 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (120 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups (270 g) semisweet chocolate chips
    Instructions:

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Grease and line an 8×8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  • Melt the butter completely.
  • Whisk the melted butter, sugar, and cocoa powder together until smooth.
  • Add the eggs one at a time, mixing after each addition.
  • Add the vanilla extract and salt, then mix until combined.
  • Fold in the flour gently until just combined. Do not overmix.
  • Stir in the chocolate chips evenly throughout the batter.
  • Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly.
  • Bake for approximately 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  • Allow brownies to cool completely before slicing.
